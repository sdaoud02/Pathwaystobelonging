About Our Program

Come spend your summer with us! Our program is designed to help newcomer children and youth integrate into the community through fun and engaging activities.

Our Services

  • Youth summer programs (ages 7-18)
  • Cultural integration activities
  • Extended Support for newcomers
  • Language support activities

Week Activity Details

  • Storytelling session on Indigenous history and culture
  • Art project: beadwork and creating cultural symbols
  • Traditional Indigenous games
