Welcome to Pathways to Belonging

Pathways to Belonging is a free summer program designed to help children and youth connect, grow, and feel a true sense of belonging in their community.

We create inclusive spaces through fun activities like:

  • Sports-based games
  • Arts & crafts
  • Field trips

🌟 Program Highlights:

Who: Children & Youth (ages 7-18)

When: Monthly Mondays (sometimes also Wednesdays) throughout summer

Cost: Completely FREE

📅 Program Schedule

Our sessions run once a week on Mondays, with some weeks including both Monday & Wednesday events. Sessions run from July 7th, 2025 – Sep 1st, 2025
